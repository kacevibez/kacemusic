About me

Odoh Kelechukwu Stanley, known by his stagename Kace is a Nigerian indigenous musician, rapper, singer and song writer.

Born 28th of march 1995 to Mr & Mrs Nicholas Odoh. Kace who is yet an unsigned artist is sure warming his way up to the heart of his fans with his hard work, consistency and great content. His tracks such as Adura, Angela and East which was the last recent realeased on august 7th 2019 has been making waves on all online and offline platforms.
